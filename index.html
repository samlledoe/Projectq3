How to Prevent Fires in Your Home

Several recent fires in the Philippines have resulted in the deaths of numerous people and the loss of refuge. March is Fire Prevention Month in the Philippines, making it an ideal opportunity to discuss and raise awareness about the causes, hazards, and dangers of fire. Electrical connections and electrical faults have been the leading cause of fires in Metro Manila, accounting for 53.85% of all fires on an annual basis, which is why we must put a stop to it.

Store any flammable products correctly. Maintain your wires. Make Sure You Have a Working Fire Extinguisher in Your Home.
Install Smoke Detectors and Test Them Regularly. Keep lit candles away from flammable materials such as cloth and wood. Turn off the stove and remove pots and pans from burner right after cooking.
